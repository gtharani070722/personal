## 🚽 Constipation Fix Protocol

Your "trying hard to poop" issue is likely due to:
- **Low water** (~1L) — increases stool hardness
- **Low fiber** — slows transit time
- **No morning movement** — bowels need activation

### Solution Stack

| Action | When | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **1 glass warm water** | Immediately on waking | Triggers gastrocolic reflex |
| **5-min walk/stretch** | Before bathroom attempt | Activates bowels |
| **Psyllium husk (isabgol)** | 1 tsp in water at night | Bulks stool; take 2–3×/week |
| **More fiber** | Throughout day | Oats, vegetables, fruits with skin |
| **Adequate water** | 2.5–3L daily | Fiber needs water to work |
| **Consistent timing** | Same time daily | Trains bowel habit |

> ✅ Within 1–2 weeks of this protocol, bathroom time should be much easier.
